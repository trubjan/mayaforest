My project

What is the purpose of my website?

My website is a modified version of the famous CSS Zen Garden project by Dave Shea. Although it's no longer possible to contribute to the online examples, I thought it would be a good opportunity to try out layout methods such as CSS Flexbox and Grid. I chose the theme of ancient Myan culture, which was the motivating force that took my husband and myself to Mexico (that was my excuse anyway).

In case you're wondering what the dots and bar mean (top left-hand corner of each page), they're the Mayan numbers 1 to 5. The video on this page is about a half-hour long, so if you're into ancient civilizations or archaeology stuff, make yourself a cuppa and settle in!
